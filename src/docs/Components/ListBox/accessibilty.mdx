## Accessibility and semantic

<HintItem>
  The attribute <strong>role="listbox"</strong> must{' '}
  <strong>always be present</strong> on the <strong>.mc-listbox</strong>{' '}
  element.
</HintItem>
<HintItem>
  The attribute <strong>role="option"</strong> must{' '}
  <strong>always be present</strong> on the all{' '}
  <strong>.mc-listbox__tile</strong> elements.
</HintItem>
<HintItem>
  The attribute <strong>aria-selected="true"</strong> must{' '}
  <strong>always be present</strong> on the all selected{' '}
  <strong>.mc-listbox__tile</strong> elements.
</HintItem>
