> Mozaic is first thought to answer to LEROY MERLIN's brand guidelines but customisation is also allowed.

## Adeo color palette

We have set Mozaic to be customisable to meet the needs of LEROY MERLIN's group: ADEO.
Adeo provides collaborative tools for its employees, and the visual identity in these tools is based on Adeo's brand colors.

With Mozaic, we provide you two different UI kits. One that is branded LEROY MERLIN and another one branded Adeo. Feel free to download the folder and explore these two UI kits.
