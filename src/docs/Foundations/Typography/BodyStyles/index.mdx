> The body style pattern provide basic typographic style for readable multilines text or paragraphs like a product description or an article.

## Body style is defined with three factors:

- Font-size _(that do not vary depending on the viewport)_
- Line-height _(that are optimised for readability and accessibility)_
- Font-weight _(regular)_
